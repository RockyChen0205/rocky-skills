# Placement Rules

Insert citations at the sentence that makes the claim, not at the end of a long paragraph recap.

Placement policy:

- Score sentences independently.
- Skip sentences that already contain a citation marker unless the task is explicitly about auditing existing references.
- Cap insertions per paragraph. Default: at most 2 accepted insertions.
- Maintain sentence spacing. Default: do not insert into immediately adjacent sentences in the same paragraph.
- Suppress rapid reuse. If the same paper was inserted in a nearby paragraph, prefer a different source unless the claim clearly requires the same citation.
- Prefer the first precise statement over trailing summary sentences.

Safe skips:

- Pure transition sentences
- Rhetorical questions
- Headings, captions, and bullet labels
- Claims too vague to search without inventing support
